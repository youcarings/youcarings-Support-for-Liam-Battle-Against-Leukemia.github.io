Medical & Health

My name is Ann Callahan and I am organizing this fundraiser on behalf of the family of little Liam Timothy. Their little boy has been battling sickness for over a month now. For the last two weeks he has had an unbroken fever, and has been absolutely pitiful. They received bloodwork last week that resulted in Liam being taken to Children's Hospital in Birmingham for further testing. It has now been confirmed that our sweet boy has Leukemia. In the short time he's been at Children's he has undergone several blood transfusions, and is going to have a bone marrow biopsy as well as a spinal tap this upcoming Monday. Liam's mom works as a hairdresser and his dad works in construction, and they will have to be in and out of work for the foreseeable future while Liam is undergoing his cancer treatments. My hope is for them to be able to focus on their sweet baby without having to worry about their finances. If it is put on your heart to donate to this family in their time of need, no amount is too small and any donation is appreciated.

Please keep all of them in your thoughts and prayers as they continue down this difficult road. Prayer's for Liam to return to his happy, strong-willed, outside loving little self. Prayers for Liam's family. And prayers for his medical team.

All funds will go towards helping with Liam's medical expenses, and helping the Timothy's pay their bills while they are unable to work.
